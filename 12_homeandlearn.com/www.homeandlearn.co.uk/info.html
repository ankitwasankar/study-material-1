Failed Downloads

Downloads can fail for any number of reasons: your internet connection cuts off, loss of power to your PC, our servers going down - the list is long. That's why we're anxious you don't miss out. If your download fails, for any reason, we'll make sure you get the books you paid for. Here's how.

Send us an Email

The first step is to get in touch with us at the following email address:

Email Address

When you send us an email, please include your 2CO order number. This is the 10 digit number that 2CO emails to you when you complete your order. They also send us the same number. So when the two match, we can be sure the order is valid.

Download your files

Once we get your email, we'll set up a special login for you. Simply login, and download your files. It's that easy!

So, remember: KEEP THIS WINDOW OPEN UNTIL YOUR PDF BOOKS HAVE BEEN DOWNLOADED AND OPENED ON YOUR PC. IF THEY FAIL - GET IN TOUCH!
