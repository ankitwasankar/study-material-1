How to Order a PDF Book

 

 

How to Order

To order a PDF book, click a checkbox on the PDF Order form. You will then see your chosen book appear in the textarea, along with the total price. When you are happy with your choice, click the big button at the bottom of the page "Pay for your Course Books on 2CO's Secure Servers". You will then be taken to the 2CO payment area, where you can enter your details. (We don't need to collect or store any details from you, as 2CO sends us an email about your order.)

If you make a mistake, simply untick a checkbox. The book will then disappear from the textarea, and the total will be adjusted.
