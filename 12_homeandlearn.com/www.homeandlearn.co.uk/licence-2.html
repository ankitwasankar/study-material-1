If you are a teacher or educator then you may be interested in our Site Licence. This grants your school, college, or further education centre permission to teach any of our courses. Before an educational institution will allow you to place our material on your servers, they need to know that all the copyright issues are taken care of - that's what our site licence does. You can download a copy below to hand to your admin teams.

The site licence works on the number of students you expect to teach, and is priced accordingly. Once everyone's happy on your side, you would send us your signed site licence, and a purchase order or cheque. We then arrange for you to download our material. Here's what you get:

  • All five Home and Learn computer courses: Visual Basic .Net, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Web Design and Web Scripting, PHP for beginners.
  • The right to place all five course on your institute's server
  • Print copies out for yourself and your students
  • Give a copy of the course to your authorised students
  • A full, highly-regarded computer course without you doing any work!

The prices for a site licence are as follows (a User can be a student or teacher):
Num of Users Price per 5 Books Example
10 to 20 users £13.50 per set of 5 books 20 users x £13.50 = £270.00
21 to 50 users £12.00 per set of 5 books 50 users x £12.00 = £600.00
51 or more users £10.00 per set of 5 books 100 users x £10.00 = £1000


The site licence is for one year, and so needs to be renewed after 12 months (we can offer discounts after the first year, though. If you have fewer than 10 students then we don't offer the licence.)
