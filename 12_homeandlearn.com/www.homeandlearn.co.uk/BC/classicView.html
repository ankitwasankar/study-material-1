Switch to Classic View

 

To see a list of Control Panel icons instead of "Pick a Category" options, you need to be in Classic View. Here's how.

  • Click the Start button, then click Control Panel on the Start menu
  • In the top left of the Control Panel, select Switch to Classic View. (If it says Switch to Category View then you're already in Classic View, and there's no need to do anything):

When you click on Switch to Classic View, you'll see the Control Panel as a list of icons:

The above icons are the ones we'll double click on throughout the Control Panel tutorials.
