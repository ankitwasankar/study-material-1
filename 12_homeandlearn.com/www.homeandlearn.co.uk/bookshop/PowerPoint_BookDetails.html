Book Details

Microsoft PowerPoint Book Details

Book Objectives
  • To take students who are new to the world of presentation (and Microsoft PowerPoint) from a beginner to an advanced stage

Book Requirements:
  • A PC, with either Windows 98, ME or XP
  • A Copy of Microsoft PowerPoint, Versions 2000 to 2003


Book Description:

This course has been designed especially for those new to the world of PowerPoint. You start off fairly slowly, and follow along with your book as you create PowerPoint presentations. Gradually, you acquire more and more skills and expertise. By the end of this book, you'll be at quite an advanced stage, with the ability to put together a professional presentation.

The PowerPoint book will cover a heck of lot of ground. And so will you. Believe in yourself and you can become a very competent PowerPoint user in no time.

Students need not have any PowerPoint experience at all before buying this book. The only thing needed for this course is a copy of Microsoft PowerPoint versions 2000 to 2003.
