Webmasters - Add a Link
 

If you have a website, and feel a reciprocal link to Home and Learn would be a good idea, then drop us a line. Things you need to consider:
 

  1. You website should have some tutorials on it
  2. It shouldn't be full of adverts
  3. It shouldn't be a directory site, with just lots of links to other sites (unless it's a REALLY good directory site)
  4. It must be a site that's relevant to ours

You also need to place a link to us, first of all. We'll then check out your site, and link (within about 1 to 2 weeks) and see if it's suitable. If it is, we'll add a link to your site. If it's not, we'll let you know why. You can then, if you prefer, delete our link on your site.
