The order process is very easy. We've teamed up with the reseller 2CO so that you can pay by credit card.

2CO handles millions of pounds worth of credit card orders every day, and are vastly experienced. Theses guys really know their stuff when it comes to security. In fact, they offer far more security than we can ever hope for alone! Which is why we teamed up with them: experience and security.

When you order a book, they will take your credit card details, and then verify that things are safe. They won't pay us a penny until we can prove we've sent you your goods! How's that for security! (If you have any doubts or questions about the order process, then we want to hear from you.)

So, if you're ready to place your order, simply click a "Buy" button in the Bookstore
